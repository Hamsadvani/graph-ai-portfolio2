About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity Bootcamp 2025 Q4 is an 8-week, hands-on program to help professionals and researchers adopt AI for productivity—no coding needed.

  • Create websites, documents, and presentations with AI support.
  • Conduct literature reviews, analyze data, and build dashboards using AI tools.
  • Design domain-specific chatbots to answer your questions.
  • Automate workflows and repetitive tasks.
  • Generate / edit images, video, and design materials with AI.
  • Integrate AI tools into your ongoing projects.

By course end, you’ll confidently use AI to save time, enhance output quality, and tackle real-world challenges in research, business, or creative domains.
