About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity Bootcamp 2025 Q4 is an 8-week hands-on program to help professionals and researchers use AI productively — no coding required.

  • Create professional websites, documents, and presentations with AI support.
  • Perform literature reviews, analyze datasets, and build dashboards with intuitive AI tools.
  • Design custom chatbots for domain-specific Q&A.
  • Automate workflows and recurring tasks.
  • Use AI for image, video, and design generation/editing.
  • Integrate AI tools into daily projects to boost impact.

By the end, you'll be able to use AI effectively to save time, raise work quality, and solve real-world challenges in research, creative, or business domains.
