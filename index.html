About Me

Dr. Hamsadvani Anand Kuganantham is a global health researcher with over 15 years of experience leading multidisciplinary research initiatives across the World Health Organization, University of Oxford, and UNICEF.

She has pioneered evidence-based policy implementation and translational research in pandemic preparedness, pathogen surveillance, and implementation science across 15+ countries. Her leadership has influenced global health policies affecting more than 70 million people.

Dr. Kuganantham currently serves as Global Research Manager at The Global Health Network, University of Oxford, where she leads six international projects spanning Africa, India, Vietnam, and the UK. She continues to bridge public health and data science, fostering equitable research capacity across LMICs.

She holds an MSc in Health Policy, Planning, and Financing from the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine and London School of Economics, and an MBBS from Dr. MGR Medical University, India.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity Bootcamp 2025 Q4 is an 8‑week hands-on program designed to help professionals, researchers, and innovators harness the power of AI to enhance productivity and accelerate real-world impact—without requiring coding expertise.

This course empowers participants to:

  • Create professional websites, documents, and presentations with AI assistance.
  • Perform literature reviews, analyze data, and build dashboards using intuitive AI tools.
  • Design custom chatbots for domain-specific applications.
  • Set up automated workflows to streamline repetitive tasks.
  • Use AI for creating and editing images, videos, and design materials.
  • Develop practical AI solutions integrated into daily work and research projects.

By the end of the program, participants will be equipped to use AI strategically—to save time, elevate work quality, and generate innovative outcomes across research, creative, and business contexts.
